I'm a guy from a small-town in Louisiana living in Dallas, TX with my wife and dog. We were scared to death to drive on the interstates when my wife and I first moved here. It took us forever to get anywhere. Now I'm a big fan of public transit and only a little bit scared of the interstates.

I like to write, draw letterforms, and build things on the web. I'm working at Kalkomey with a lot of smart people building experiences to teach people how to be safe while having fun.

Contact

Email is the best way to contact me, but I'm also on Twitter almost every day. Or if you want to grab a beer, just let me know. I'm happy to talk about whatever.
