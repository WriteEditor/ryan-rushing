I’m a guy from a small-town in Louisiana living in Dallas, TX with my wife and dog. We were scared to death to drive on the interstates when we first moved here. It took us forever to get anywhere. Now I’m a big fan of public transit and only a little bit scared of the interstates.

I’m working at Kalkomey doing design, front-end development, and animation with a lot of smart people building experiences to teach people how to be safe while having fun. Take a look at my work, or if you’re looking for more folks check out my friends and recommendations.

Graduating college was a big deal for my family and me; I’m the first college graduate, and my sisters weren’t far behind me. I was in the marching band for four years, and even joined a marching band service organization. As much as college guided my growth as a person, my web skills are almost entirely self-taught or learned from a mentor. By working with people smarter than me, reading blogs, building and rebuilding personal projects, and most recently, subscribing to Code School, I am exploring new technologies, tools, and techniques.

Contact

The best way to contact me is through Twitter and email (get@ryan-rushing.com), or we can just grab a beer or something. I’m happy to talk about whatever.
