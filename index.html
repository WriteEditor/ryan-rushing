When I was in high school a friend told me he could eat an apple in five bites.

“All of it?” I asked.

“Yep,” he replied as his teeth gripped one side of the apple.

He struggled with chewing each bite, but sure enough, he did it. Ever since then, I eat the whole apple… though I usually take smaller bites.

I'm a designer, front-end developer, and I also draw lettering, screenprint, and sculpt. I'm always interested in new things and ideas, whether that means learning a programming language or trying out a new way to eat apples.
