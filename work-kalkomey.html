  • Interactive Learning Objects

    The interactive learning objects are sprinkled throughout our courses and provide an alternate way for students to learn the content.

    I lead this project—wow that’s great to type—and I work with our subject-matter experts and design, development, and content teams on everything from concepting to design to execution.

    They can be viewed in the course or in a state’s study guide. (The ones marked “animation”.) This project is far from complete, we’re only just beginning.

  • Bookstore

    The Bookstore is where you can get digital versions of our handbooks and student manuals covering boating, hunting, and handgun safety. They match our printed publications used in classrooms.

    Through the power of infinity, you can enjoy perusing laws and regulations at any time, until your battery dies.

  • Courses’ homepage redesign

    The homepages of Boat Ed, Hunter Ed, etc. were in need of some love to align with our design, front-end development, and SEO guidelines. The project was fairly straightforward; the hardest part was organizing the new content.

    This layout was applied to all the courses’ homepages.
