  • Peeper’s

    Unit Interactive

    Peeper’s is one of the oldest (if not the oldest) boutique luxury eyeglass shops in Dallas, located in Highland Park Village. They do not deal in bulk nor sell online, but rather, they make expert recommendations based on the individual. Also, when you bring your prescription in, Peeper’s actually grinds the lenses in-house to fit your frames perfectly.

    As someone who appreciates handmade stuff, I thought that was the coolest thing; apparently not many eyeglass shops do that anymore. They were an absolute joy to work with.

    Roles

    • project manager
    • art director
    • design
    • front-end development

  • Can you define love?

    Personal work

    Can you define love? is my longest-running personal project (since 2009). It has taken many forms, but has always been a collection of love definitions provided by people I interview and passers-by on the Internet.

    The current, and most logical, iteration is now on Tumblr (using a free theme). In the past I spent lots of time on the web development/design, and paid little respect to the content, but since Tumblr is largely accessed through users’ dashboard, it gives me no excuse to misdirect my attention back to the Look and Feel™.
