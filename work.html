  • Can you define love?

    Personal work

    Can you define love? is my longest-running personal project (since 2009). It has taken many forms, but has always been a collection of love definitions provided by people I interview and Internet travellers.

    The current, and most logical, iteration is now on Tumblr (using a lightly developed theme). In the past, I spent lots of time on the web development/design, and paid little respect to the content, but since Tumblr is largely accessed through users’ dashboard, and few users see the landing page of a website, I’m forced to focus on the content.

    • concept
    • maintenance
